A problem has been encountered and ArcOS has been shut down to prevent damage
to your computer.



If this is the first time you've seen this stop error sreen,
restart your computer. If this screen appears again, follow
these steps:

Check to make sure any new software is properly loaded, and try to unload
any unnecessary applications, since they are allowed to trigger a bluescreen.
If problems continue, try to reset ArcOSv1. To do this, enter "localStorage.clear();new PowerLogic().logoff();" in the "Execute Command" application.

Technical information (provided by process that triggered the new ErrorLogic().bsod):



ArcOSv1 will restart in a couple of seconds.
